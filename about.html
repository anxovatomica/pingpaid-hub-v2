The Problem: I was a freelancer who needed to send an invoice. I searched "free invoice generator" and every tool had a catch. Sign up. Watermark. Limited to 3 invoices. $9/month after trial. Export as PDF only on premium. I just wanted to send a PDF invoice. That's it.

The Solution: I built my own. Took 2 hours. It worked. I used it for a year. Then I thought — maybe other freelancers have this problem too. So I made it open source, added more tools, and put it online for free.

💡 Our Philosophy

  • Free means free. No signup. No trial. No "freemium." Just free.
  • Simple means simple. No dashboard. No onboarding. No learning curve. Open the page and use it.
  • Open means open. All code on GitHub. Fork it. Modify it. Host it yourself. No lock-in.
  • Private means private. Your data never leaves your browser. We don't want it.

🛠️ What We Built

7 free tools:

  • Invoice Generator — Professional PDF in 30 seconds
  • Late Fee Calculator — Exact late fee breakdown
  • Hourly Rate Calculator — What should you charge?
  • Break-Even Calculator — When do you hit profit?
  • Interest Calculator — Simple + compound interest
