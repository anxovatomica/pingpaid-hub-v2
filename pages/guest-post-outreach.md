## Outreach Strategy

### Phase 1: Warm-Up (Week 1-2)
1. Comment on 3-5 blog posts per target blog
2. Share their content on Twitter/X with meaningful additions
3. Sign up for their newsletter, reply to 1-2 emails

### Phase 2: Pitch (Week 3-4)
1. Send personalized pitch using templates above
2. Reference specific posts you've read and enjoyed
3. Offer 3 topic options

### Phase 3: Write (Week 5-6)
1. Write high-quality, original content
2. Include 2-3 internal links to their existing content
3. Add one subtle link to your site (in bio or relevant context)
4. Include original data, screenshots, or examples

### Phase 4: Promote (After Publication)
1. Share on all your social channels
2. Link to the guest post from your own blog
3. Comment on the post with additional insights
4. Email your list about the post
