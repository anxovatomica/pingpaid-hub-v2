🔍 Google Search Console Setup Steps

  1. Go to https://search.google.com/search-console
  2. Click "Add Property" → choose "URL prefix"
  3. Enter: https://anxovatomica.github.io/pingpaid-hub-v2/
  4. Choose verification method: "HTML tag"
  5. Copy the meta tag (looks like: <meta name="google-site-verification" content="abc123...">)
  6. Paste it into index.html in the <head> section (replacing google-site-verification=TO_BE_ADDED)
  7. Commit and push → wait for GitHub Pages to deploy
  8. Click "Verify" in Google Search Console
  9. Go to Sitemaps → submit: https://anxovatomica.github.io/pingpaid-hub-v2/sitemap.xml
  10. Go to URL Inspection → inspect https://anxovatomica.github.io/pingpaid-hub-v2/ → click "Request Indexing"
