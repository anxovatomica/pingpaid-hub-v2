Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

TL;DR: We don't collect, store, or share your data. Everything happens in your browser. We don't use cookies, analytics, or tracking. We don't even have a server.

1. Information We Don't Collect

PingPaid does not collect, store, or process any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names or email addresses
  • Invoice data or financial information
  • IP addresses or location data
  • Browser history or cookies
  • Device information
  • Usage analytics

2. How Our Tools Work

All PingPaid tools are client-side applications. This means:

  • All calculations happen in your browser
  • Invoice data never leaves your device
  • PDFs are generated locally in your browser
  • No data is sent to our servers (we don't have any)

3. GitHub Pages Hosting

Our tools are hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect basic server logs (IP addresses, access times) for operational purposes. We do not have access to these logs. For GitHub's privacy practices, see GitHub's Privacy Statement.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, advertising, or social media widgets. The only external links on our pages are to:

  • Our GitHub repositories (for the open source code)
  • Our blog and other tools

5. Children's Privacy

Our tools are intended for general use. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions, open an issue on our GitHub repository.

Built by a freelancer who believes privacy is a right, not a feature. Open source on GitHub.
